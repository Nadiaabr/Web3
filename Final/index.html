Keep your back healthy

Back pain is an epidemic among office workers. Studies show that employees with a long history of back pain received 32% of improvement in the lower back pain after using standing desks for several weeks.

More energy for your work

Standing at your desk elevates your energy levels. According to a 7-week study, participants using standing reported less stress and fatigue than those who stayed seated during the work day.
